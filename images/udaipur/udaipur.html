Udaipur

Discover the Venice of the East, a city of shimmering lakes and romantic palaces.

Attractions

Visit the iconic City Palace, enjoy boat rides on Lake Pichola, and explore serene gardens.

Food

Enjoy romantic lakeside dining and savor authentic Mewari cuisine and street food.

Shopping

Find exquisite miniature paintings, leather goods, and colorful Rajasthani crafts.

Must-Visit Attractions

View on Maps

City Palace

A magnificent complex of palaces on the banks of Lake Pichola, the City Palace is a testament to the splendor of the Mewar dynasty. It offers panoramic views of the lake and the city.

View on Maps

Lake Pichola

The heart of Udaipur, this artificial freshwater lake is home to the famous island palaces of Jag Mandir and Jag Niwas (now the Taj Lake Palace). A boat ride here, especially at sunset, is an unforgettable experience.

Famous Food Places

Ambrai Restaurant

Offers a magical dining experience with stunning views of the City Palace and Lake Pichola. Perfect for a romantic evening.

View on Maps

Natraj Dining Hall

A local institution famous for its unlimited and authentic Rajasthani and Gujarati thalis served at lightning speed.

View on Maps

Paliwal Restaurant

A small, bustling local spot famous for its delicious and crispy Pyaaz and Dal Kachoris.

View on Maps

Shopping Highlights

Hathi Pol Bazaar

Famous for its exquisite miniature paintings, a traditional art form of the region. You can also find handicrafts, wooden toys, and colorful textiles, making it a great place to buy souvenirs.
