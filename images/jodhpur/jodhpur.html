Jodhpur

Experience the majestic Blue City, watched over by the imposing Mehrangarh Fort.

Attractions

Explore one of India's largest forts, serene marble cenotaphs, and grand palaces.

Food

Taste the famous Makhaniya Lassi, Mawa Kachori, and authentic Marwari cuisine.

Shopping

Buy traditional Jodhpuri juttis, spices, and beautiful handicrafts at Sardar Market.

Must-Visit Attractions

View on Maps

Mehrangarh Fort

Towering over the city, this magnificent fort is one of the largest in India. It houses several palaces known for their intricate carvings and expansive courtyards, offering breathtaking views of the blue city below.

View on Maps

Jaswant Thada

Often called the "Taj Mahal of Marwar," this is a stunning cenotaph built of intricately carved sheets of white marble. The serene atmosphere and beautiful architecture make it a must-visit.

Famous Food Places

Mishrilal Hotel

Located at the Clock Tower, this legendary spot has been serving the best Makhaniya Lassi for decades.

View on Maps

Janta Sweet Home

The go-to place for trying Jodhpur's famous Mawa Kachori, along with a huge variety of other local sweets and snacks.

View on Maps

Gypsy Restaurant

Famous for its massive and delicious Rajasthani thali, offering a taste of numerous local dishes in a single meal.

View on Maps

Shopping Highlights

Sardar Market

Located near the Clock Tower, this bustling market is the perfect place to shop for spices, textiles, silver jewelry, and handicrafts. It provides a vibrant glimpse into the local life and culture of Jodhpur.
