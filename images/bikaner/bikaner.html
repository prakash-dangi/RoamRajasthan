Bikaner

Explore the Camel Country, a vibrant desert town known for its fort, temples, and savory snacks.

Attractions

Discover the magnificent Junagarh Fort, the unique Karni Mata Temple, and camel research centers.

Food

Taste the world-famous Bikeneri Bhujia and indulge in a variety of traditional sweets and snacks.

Shopping

Shop for camel leather goods, local handicrafts, and the delicious snacks the city is famous for.

Must-Visit Attractions

View on Maps

Junagarh Fort

An imposing fort in the heart of Bikaner, Junagarh is unique as it's not built on a hilltop. It's a formidable structure with a rich history, beautiful palaces, temples, and pavilions inside.

View on Maps

Karni Mata Temple

Located in Deshnoke, this famous and unique temple is also known as the "Temple of Rats." Thousands of holy rats, called kabbas, are believed to be reincarnated devotees and are revered by pilgrims.

Famous Food Places

Chhotu Motu Joshi

A legendary sweet shop that is a must-visit for tasting authentic Bikeneri Bhujia and a wide variety of other local sweets.

View on Maps

Laxmi Niwas Palace

Experience royal dining in a magnificent heritage hotel. It offers a luxurious ambiance and a taste of the royal cuisine of Bikaner.

View on Maps

Heeralal's

A popular local chain known for its high-quality sweets, snacks, and casual dining options. A reliable choice for good food.

View on Maps

Shopping Highlights

Kote Gate

The main entrance to the old city, the area around Kote Gate is a bustling market famous for camel-hide goods, local handicrafts, and of course, Bikaner's signature snacks.
