Jaisalmer

Step into a fairy tale in the heart of the Thar Desert, the stunning Golden City.

Attractions

Explore the living fort, intricate havelis, and the vast expanse of the Sam Sand Dunes.

Food

Enjoy rooftop dining with fort views and taste unique local sweets like Ghotua Laddoos.

Shopping

Shop for authentic leather goods, camel souvenirs, and beautiful Rajasthani puppets.

Must-Visit Attractions

View on Maps

Jaisalmer Fort (Sonar Quila)

A massive sandstone fort that seems to rise from the desert. Unlike most forts, it's a "living fort," with thousands of people residing within its walls, amidst shops, hotels, and ancient havelis.

View on Maps

Patwon Ki Haveli

A cluster of five magnificent havelis, this is the most elaborate and impressive in Jaisalmer. Its ornate architecture, with intricate mirror-work and paintings, offers a glimpse into the opulent lifestyle of its former residents.

Famous Food Places

Trio Restaurant

One of the city's most renowned restaurants, offering authentic Rajasthani cuisine with stunning rooftop views of the fort.

View on Maps

Dhanraj Ranmal Bhatia Sweets

A historic sweet shop famous for its local specialty, Ghotua Laddoos, a must-try when in Jaisalmer.

View on Maps

Kuku Coffee Shop

A charming little cafe offering great coffee and snacks with one of the best, unobstructed views of the Jaisalmer Fort.

View on Maps

Shopping Highlights

Sadar Bazaar

A vibrant wholesale and retail market where you can find everything from leather goods, especially camel leather products, to traditional jewelry, puppets, and colorful Rajasthani textiles.
