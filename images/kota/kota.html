Kota

The industrial capital of Rajasthan, beautifully situated on the banks of the Chambal River.

Attractions

Discover majestic palaces, serene gardens, and unique parks along the Chambal River.

Food

Indulge in the city's famous savory snack, Kota Kachori, and other local delicacies.

Shopping

Shop for the world-renowned, lightweight Kota Doria sarees and local handicrafts.

Must-Visit Attractions

View on Maps

Seven Wonders Park

See mini replicas of the world’s seven wonders—Taj Mahal, Eiffel Tower, Great Pyramid and more—lined along the edge of Kishore Sagar Lake. A popular sunset spot and great for photos.

View on Maps

Garh Palace (City Palace)

A sprawling palace complex with beautiful archways, frescoed rooms, and the Maharao Madho Singh Museum. It offers a fascinating glimpse into the region's royal history and art.

Famous Food Places

Sualalji's Kota Kachori

The most iconic spot in the city to try the legendary Kota Kachori, a spicy and savory local snack.

Amar Punjabi Dhaba

A favorite among locals for authentic and delicious North Indian and Punjabi cuisine in a lively setting.

Jodhpur Misthan Bhandar

Famous for its wide variety of traditional Rajasthani sweets, namkeens, and other savory snacks.

Shopping Highlights

Kota Doria Sarees

Kota is famous for its unique Kota Doria fabric, a lightweight blend of cotton and silk. Head to the markets near Kaithoon to buy authentic sarees and dress materials directly from the weavers.
