Jaipur

The vibrant capital of Rajasthan, famously known as the Pink City for its stunning historic architecture.

Attractions

Witness the grandeur of royal palaces, majestic forts, and bustling historic markets.

Food

Savor the iconic Pyaaz Kachori, Ghewar, and a rich tapestry of Rajasthani thalis.

Shopping

Explore bustling bazaars for exquisite jewelry, vibrant textiles, and unique handicrafts.

Must-Visit Attractions

View on Maps

Hawa Mahal

An iconic pink sandstone palace with 953 intricately designed windows. This "Palace of Winds" was built to allow royal women to observe street festivities unseen from the outside.

View on Maps

Amer Fort

A majestic fort located on a hilltop, known for its artistic Hindu style elements. Explore its cobbled paths, large ramparts, and the stunning Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace).

Famous Food Places

Laxmi Misthan Bhandar

A legendary restaurant in the heart of the old city, famous for its traditional Rajasthani thali and sweets like Ghewar.

View on Maps

Rawat Misthan Bhandar

Home to the original and most famous Pyaaz Kachori in Jaipur. A must-visit for any food lover.

View on Maps

Masala Chowk

An open-air food court that brings together the best of Jaipur's street food in one vibrant location.

View on Maps

Shopping Highlights

Bapu Bazaar

One of the most famous markets in Jaipur, known for its vibrant atmosphere and a wide array of Rajasthani textiles, handicrafts, and jutis (traditional footwear).
